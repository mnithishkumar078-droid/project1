About OnlineVoting

Empowering Democracy Through Technology

Our Mission

OnlineVoting is dedicated to making the democratic process more accessible, secure, and transparent. We believe that every citizen should have the opportunity to participate in elections conveniently from anywhere in the world, while maintaining the highest standards of security and integrity.

How It Works

Our platform uses Aadhaar e-KYC for voter verification, ensuring that each eligible citizen can vote only once. The entire process is transparent and auditable, with real-time result counting and publication.
